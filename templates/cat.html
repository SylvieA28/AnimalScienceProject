Tips to Take Care

  • Groom your cat regularly
  • Provide fresh water 24/7 to avoid common health issues
  • Cats usually prefer moving water, like a small fountain
  • Have enough litter boxes for your cat - get the amount of cats you have + 1 (so if you have 2 cats, get 3 litter boxes)
  • Get a scratchpost so the cat doesn't destroy the furniture
  • When in a car, keep the cat in a cat carrier
  • Spraying or neutering your cat not only prevents babies, it also prevents certain diseases
  • Visit the vet at least once a year
