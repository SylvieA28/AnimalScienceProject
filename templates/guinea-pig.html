Tips to Take Care

  • When replacing bedding, put your guinea pigs in a carrier, then dump the old bedding, wipe out the inside of the cage, and replace the bedding
  • Give 1/2 cup of food per guinea pig
  • Play with them gently, they startle and get injured easily
  • Annual checkups are recommended
  • Make sure they have enough vitamin C
  • Give an unlimited amount of green grass hay but limit other greens since they can cause health issues
  • For a single guinea pig, the cage should be a mimimum of 24"L, 24"W, 15"H
  • For multiple, a minimum floor size of 24" by 36"
  • Aspen or kiln-dried pine shaves are great, inexpensive bedding
