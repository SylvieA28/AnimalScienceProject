Tips to Take Care

  • When cleaning the coop (about once a month), remove the old wood shavings, sweep out the cage, and scoop up any bedding or poop from the food
  • Scatter dried mealworms all around the cage for them to eat
  • Make a minimum of 2 - 3 square feet per chicken
  • Make 1 nexting box per 3 hens to lay their eggs, but the more the better
  • Let them free range to keep them happier, have to buy less store bought grit (hard substance that helps with digestion and keeps bones + egg shells strong), contain illnesses, and more
  • Even if they're free range, provide ample egg shell grit
  • They eat a varied diet but avoid chocolate, avocados, onions, garlic, citrus fruits, and highly processed or salty foods
  • Check the chicken's health frequently
