Tips to Take Care

  • Talk to them every day, they're very social
  • The bird's habitat should be at least 18 inches in width, length, and height but always give them as much space possible
  • Avoid wooden habitats or galvanized wire
  • They need exposure to sunlight so take them outside once in a while
  • Give the parakeet lots of toys to play with
  • Put wet food, dry food, and water in all different bowls. If you have multiple parakeets, each should have their own bowl
  • Give them cuttlebones, a great source of Calcium
  • Only use chemicals approved for pets when cleaning cage
