Tips to Take Care

  • DONT FEED YOUR DOG CHOCOLATE
  • Dogs need lots of walks, make sure to be ready to take them out daily
  • Dogs aren't like cats and dont poop in a litterbox, they do it outside. Make sure someone is around to pick up after it
  • Make sure to train your dog so it doesn't behave badly
  • Have lots of space for it to jump around without ruining the house
  • Groom the dog frequently to keep them clean and pest free
  • Moniter food consumption so that the dog doesn't become too fat
  • Schedule annual checkups with vet and vaccinate
  • Brush the dog's teeth
