Tips to Take Care

  • When cleaning the cages, put the bunnies in the same place
  • If you have a male and female bunny, keep them seperate
  • Throw out the old bedding in the cage (just enough to fill the bottom) and disinfect it with wipes or spray
  • Give each bunny 1/2 a cup of pellet food and hay each
  • Constant supply of fresh water needed
  • Grass hay should be available at all times
  • Sign up for routine checkups
  • Rabbits enjoy sweets but don't give too many
  • Give them tunnels and more to keep them entertained
