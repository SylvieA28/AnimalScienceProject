Tips to Take Care

  • Make sure you have at least two due to social nature
  • Always give the finch access to food and clean water
  • Give the bird a source of calcium like a chicken egg shell or sea shells
  • The mimimum cage size is 24"W,16"H,16"L
  • They love bathing so make sure the water dish is large enough to let them bathe in
  • Perches are a must
