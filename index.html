Welcome students!

In this site, you will be learning about the different animals

in this class. Unfortunately, you can't touch them since you can

hurt them or yourself. Hopefully this interactive experience will

be just as fun. Happy learning!

Instructions

You'll have to follow a few steps before starting each game.

  1. Click on the settings button on the top left
  2. Scroll all the way down
  3. Click on the switch labeled "Display Server Graphics Screen"
  4. Click on the layout button on the bottom left
  5. Click on the button labeled "Fullscreen Graphics Output"
  6. Now you're ready to play the game!
